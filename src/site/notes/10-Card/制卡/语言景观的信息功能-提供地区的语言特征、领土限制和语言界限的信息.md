Consistent use of a single language within the linguistic landscape of a territorycan contribute to clear-cut language boundaries between adjoining language groups in a given geographical region. Well-established language boundaries can stabilize relations between rival language groups by clearly delineating the administrative territories where members of the language group can expect to use and receive government and private sector services in their own language.
